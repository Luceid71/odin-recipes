White Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Do you love white chocolate? Do you love cookies? If so, you'll love white chocolate chip cookies!

This recipe mixes a smooth white chocolate flavor with your tried a true cookie recipe for a delicious treat!

Ingredients

  • White chocolate chips
  • Two and 1/2 cups white flour
  • Two Eggs
  • Two sticks butter
  • One cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • Two teaspoons salt
  • One teaspoon vanilla extract
  • One teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Melt two sticks of butter in the microwave or in a pot on the stove
  2. Mix the melted butter with the two eggs in a large bowl
  3. Mix in the salt, vannila extract, and baking powder, and stir until the mixture is smooth
  4. Mix in the brown and white sugar, and stir until the mixture is smooth
  5. Mix in the white flour 1/2 cup at a time, each time stiring until the mixture is smooth
  6. Mix in the white chocolate chips until your desired ratio is met
  7. Spoon the batter out onto cooking trays, in cookie sized blobs
  8. Cook in the over at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes, optionally switching the trays at the 10 minute mark to ensure even cooking
  9. Remove trays from oven, and let cool for 10 minutes
  10. Enjoy, and make sure to lick the cookie dough from the bowl!
