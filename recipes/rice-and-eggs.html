Rice and Eggs

Description

Do you need a fast and healthy mainstay meal, for when you don't really feel like cooking? If so, you just might love rice and eggs!

This recipe uses easy ingredients for a simple meal with low effort and fast prep time! A rice cooker is essential for this meal's fast and effortless preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 to 3/4 cup rice per person
  • 1-3 eggs per person
  • Any topppings you like, for example salt, pepper, or cayenne pepper for some added spiciness
  • Rice cooker

Instructions

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker 30-40 minutes before meal prep
  2. Fry or boil eggs
  3. Add rice and eggs to a bowl and sprinkle any desired toppings
  4. Enjoy!
